All About Offcanvas
Offcanvas
  • This is an Offcanvas.
  • Offcanvas is a sidebar component that can be toggled via JavaScript to appear from the left, right, top, or bottom edge of the viewport.
  • Buttons or anchors are used as triggers that are attached to specific elements you toggle, and data attributes are used to invoke our JavaScript.
  • To close, click on the X on the sidebar, or anywhere on oncanvas.
Offcanvas

I will not close if you click outside of me.

This is done with data-bs-backdrop="static"

Click 'X' to exit.

Offcanvas with body scrolling
  • This Offcanvas lets you scroll while the sidebar is open.
  • Try scrolling the rest of the page to see this option in action.
  • Notice you cannot click inside the webpage to close.
  • You must X to close
  • Once closed, notice that you are brought back to where you started when you opended the sidebar
Offcanvas Top
  • Look, you can even have the offcanvas drop from the top
  • But you can only have one sidebar open at one time
  • Try scrolling now. Without data-bs-scroll="true" you cannot scroll
  • But you can close the sidebar by clicking anywhere on the page. Try it now!
Offcanvas right

Yes, you can have it come from the right as well

This is done with class="offcanvas offcanvas-end"

Offcanvas bottom

Yup, you guessed it. It can even come from the bottom. Question: Can you guess what class is used for this offcanvas?
