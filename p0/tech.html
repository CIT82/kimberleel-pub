Sound Mixing

Unleash your creativity and take your sonic explorations to the next level in this class focused on mixing and synthesizing. Mixing becomes your ultimate sound-shaping tool. You'll learn to manipulate frequencies, dynamics, and effects to transform raw recordings into polished, impactful mixes. Think of yourself as a musical sculptor, refining every element to achieve your desired soundscape.
