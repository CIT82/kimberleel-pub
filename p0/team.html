Our Team

Our instructors are the heart and soul of Offbeat Studios. More than just teachers, they are passionate musicians, dedicated mentors, and inspiring guides. With years of experience performing and teaching, they understand the joy of music and know how to unlock your potential. They'll tailor lessons to match your skill level, goals, and the music you love. Expect a welcoming, supportive environment where learning is fun and your musical journey is celebrated!

Meet The Instructors

Wayne Ponce
Rock Band

A veteran of the local music scene, Wayne brings years of performing experience to his teaching. From classic rock to modern metal, he'll guide you through the fundamentals of playing with a group while helping you develop your own unique sound.

Tabitha Potter
Keyboard

Tabitha Potter believes anyone can rock! Her patient approach and easy-to-follow lessons make learning the keyboard fun and stress-free for students of all ages. Get ready to discover the joy of making music with Tabitha.

Alex Ramirez
Drums

Alex lives and breathes the beat! His infectious energy and deep knowledge of drumming styles will help you master rock-solid timing, funky syncopation, and everything in between.

Charles King
Guitar

Charles knows that learning guitar should be a blast. His patient instruction and focus on the joy of guitar make him the perfect guide for beginners and experienced players alike.

Candy Yip
Voice

With her powerful voice and background in classical technique, Candy guides her students to discover their full vocal potential. Whether you dream of singing opera arias or belting out pop hits, she'll help you develop a strong foundation and healthy vocal habits.

Marcus Evans
Bass

From funk to jazz to metal, Marcus Evans has explored it all. He'll share his wide-ranging musical knowledge and help you develop your own unique style on the bass.

Jenna show
Sound Production

Jenna Shaw sees sound production as a partnership. She'll work with you to understand your artistic vision and help you translate it into polished recordings.

James Jackson
Sound Mixing

James turns musical ideas into sonic realities. He'll guide you through the world of recording software, mixing techniques, and the art of crafting the perfect sound.
